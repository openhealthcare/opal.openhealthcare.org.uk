OPAL

A highly customisable framework for building clinical applications

Clinical-need centered design

Our strong focus on the user needs of the clinical end user, along with our insistence on deep clinical engagement at every stage makes services built on top of OPAL are a joy to use.

Customisation

We believe that no two clinical services are the same, and that the digital services that support them need to reflect that. We provide complete customisation services to create a bespoke application that tailors the wealth of features available in OPAL to your unique situation.

Supporting your services

We're in it for the long haul. We want to build long-term partnerships with organisations looking to build the best possible digital environment for healthcare in the 21st century. With a wide range of support services, whether you want the assurance of knowing someone you trust will pick up the phone, or just some pointers to get you started - we've got your back.

Understanding the NHS

We know how organisations within the NHS function. OPAL has been deployed and certified compliant for Information Governance and Clinical Safety and we know how to get that in place at your organization.

OPAL is ready to integrate with the rest of your infrastructure using Open APIs and Open Standards.

Open All The Things

OPAL is entirely Open Source, Open Governance. Vendor neutrality is baked in to the licenses we provide at a fundamental level. Our customers are always free to customise and re-use the work with other partners.
